The Importance of Travel Insurance

Traveling should be an enjoyable and stress-free experience. However, unexpected situations such as medical emergencies, lost baggage, or flight cancellations can turn a trip into a financial burden. Travel insurance is designed to protect travelers from such unforeseen events.

Why You Need Travel Insurance

  • Medical Expenses – Covers hospital bills, treatments, and emergency evacuations.
  • Trip Cancellations – Get reimbursed for prepaid travel expenses if your plans change unexpectedly.
  • Baggage Loss or Delay – Provides compensation for lost, stolen, or delayed luggage.
  • Travel Delays – Covers additional expenses due to transportation interruptions.
